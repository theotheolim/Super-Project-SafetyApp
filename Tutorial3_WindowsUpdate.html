Welcome to Day 3!!

We will learn how to update your Windows(Computer) in this lesson.

Updating could be the most basic step you can take to prevent security threats.

This is important because outdated devices are more prone to viruses / security issues.

First, locate the search bar next to your windows icon.

If you can't see the search bar, press the Windows key on your keyboard.

Then type "settings" in your search bar. Click Open.

In settings, you'll see another search bar on the left.

Type "Update" to find "Windows update settings". Click on it.

Now you can check if your device is up to date. Click the update button if it says the update is available.
