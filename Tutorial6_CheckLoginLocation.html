Welcome to Day 6!!

Today we will learn about checking device and account login locations. This will be seen using a Google Account as an example.

Checking device and account login locations will allow you to take action if and unrecognized device is using your account.

Doing this every so often will ensure an extra measure of security in case your account was breached.

First, go to the Google homepage. Click on your profile icon on the top right corner.

Then click "Manage your Google Account".

Click "Security" on the left.

Scroll down until you see "Manage All Devices"

Here you can see where and when your account was used and which devices had access to it.

If there is an unrecognized location or device, immediately change your password, as you learned in Day 4.

Doing this will log out your account on every single device, preventing data access from unauthorized people.

REMINDER: Check the log in locations frequently to prevent any data breach!
