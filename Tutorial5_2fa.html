Welcome to Day 5!!

We will learn about 2 factor authentication. We will use a Google account as an example.

2 factor authentication is an extra step of verification one needs to complete when logging into an account.

Once set up, it will send a verification code to your phone number or an alternative email.

That way, only YOU will be able to sign into your account.

First, go to the Google homepage. Click on your profile icon on the top right corner.

Then click "Manage your Google Account".

Click "Security" on the left.

Click on "2-step verification".

We will set up a verification with your phone number. Click on "Phone number".

Enter your phone number. Click next and move onto the next page.

You'll receive a code on your phone. Type this code into the Google page you were in.

From now on, when you're logging into your account, Google will send you a verification code to your phone.
