Welcome to Day 7!!

Today we will learn how to check and update your account privacy settings to ensure your personal information remains secure.

You don't want strangers seeing your social media posts on the internet, as it may reveal your personal information.

Using your account's privacy settings correctly can help you stay safe!

Doing this keeps your information safe and keeps you away from unwanted attention on the internet.

Limiting access to your social media accounts can usually be accessed in settings on your social media app.

In settings, locate "Account Privacy".

Toggle the "Private Account" switch. Now people have to send a request in order to follow your account and see your posts.

In settings, you may also control the people you've blocked.

Moreover, you may also control more interactions with other users through the "How others interact with you" page.

Check your followers and who you follow frequently. Watch out for strangers!

If you find an unrecognized person on your social media, consider blocking them.

REMINDER: Only let PEOPLE YOU KNOW have access to your social media posts! Stay Safe!
