Welcome to Day 2!!

We will learn about App Permissions in this lesson.

Setting up the apps' permissions will allow you to edit WHAT your apps can access.

For example, your instagram app may access your camera, microphone, and location.

Setting App Permissions is IMPORTANT because sometimes you don't want certain apps accessing your information.

First, locate your Settings app on your device.

Second, click on "Search". You might have to scroll up to find this.

Third, search up the app you would like to edit.

Now you'll see what the app can access. Click on the green switch next to each permission to allow or remove permission.
