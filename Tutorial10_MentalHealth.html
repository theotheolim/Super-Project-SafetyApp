Welcome to Day 10!!

While electronics allow for maintaining relationships over long distances and make life easier as a whole, there are also negative effects associated with using electronics. This lesson will overview 3 potential negative effects of the online world and give tips on how to combat them.

REMINDER: Spending too much time on the online world is not good!

Spend time with friends and family outside away from your computer or phone.

Overuse of social media can lead to feelings of inadequacy, loneliness, and anxiety due to comparison with others.

To combat this be selective about the content you consume. Follow accounts that promote positivity, and unfollow accounts that induce stress or negativity.

Excessive screen time, especially before bed, can interfere with sleep quality and patterns.

A fix for this is to limit screen time by setting specific hours for device use. Use apps that track and manage your screen time. Avoid using your phone before bed.

Constant notifications and multitasking can impair focus.

To solve this problem designate areas in your home, like the bedroom or dining area, as tech-free zones to encourage healthier interactions and improve sleep. Especially when focusing on tasks such as studying remember to stay away from electronics.

Remember that anything in excess could be bad for you including electronics.

While there may be downsides you can be responsible with electronic usage and turn the online world into a positive impact on your life!
