Welcome to Day 7!!

Today we will learn how to check and update your account privacy settings to ensure your personal information remains secure.

Using your account's privacy settings correctly can help you stay safe on the internet!

Doing this keeps you away from unwantd attention on the internet and keeps your personal information safe.

Limiting access to your social media accounts can usually be accessed in settings on your social media app.

In settings, locate "Account Privacy".

Switch on the "Private Account" switch.

In settings, you may also control blocked personel.

In settings, you may also control more interaction with other users through the "How others interact with you" page.

Check your followers and following frequently. Watch out for strangers!

If you find an unrecognized person on your social media consider blocking them.

REMINDER: Only let people you know have access to your social media accounts! Stay Safe!
