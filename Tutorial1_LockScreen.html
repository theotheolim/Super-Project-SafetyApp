Welcome to Day 1!!

We will learn about the Lock Screen in this lesson.

Setting up the lock screen will secure your phone and require a passcode before entering onto your phone.

For instance, if someone were to steal your phone, they would need to know the passcode before accessing your apps and information.

Setting up a passcode ensures an extra level of security to protect your private information from anyone who is able to use your phone.

First, locate your Settings app on your device.

Second, click on "Search". You might have to scroll up to find this.

Third, search up "passcode," and click on "Face ID & Passcode."

Scroll down until you see "Turn Passcode ON" and click it.

Now you can either choose a 6-digit passcode or click "Passcode Options" to change the passcode type.

Whichever method works best, you can choose.
